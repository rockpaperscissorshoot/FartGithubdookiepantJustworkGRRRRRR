My Chip List !

Tortitos

Tortitos tortilla chips are crispy, crunchy, and perfect for dipping in your favorite salsa or guacamole. Made from high-quality corn, these chips are seasoned to perfection and offer a delightful snacking experience.

Doritos

Doritos are a popular brand of flavored tortilla chips produced since 1964. Known for their bold flavors and satisfying crunch, Doritos come in a variety of flavors, including the classic Nacho Cheese and Cool Ranch. Perfect for snacking on their own or adding a flavorful crunch to your favorite recipes.

Lonestar Tortilla Chips

Lonestar Tortilla Chips bring the authentic taste of Texas to your snack time. These chips are made from stone-ground corn and cooked to a perfect crisp. With a hint of sea salt, they are ideal for enjoying on their own or with your favorite dip.

Mission Tortilla Chips

Mission Tortilla Chips are known for their authentic Mexican flavor and perfect crunch. Made from 100% whole grain corn, these chips are gluten-free and have no artificial flavors or preservatives. They are perfect for dipping in salsa, guacamole, or enjoying on their own.
